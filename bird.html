Bird Health & Fitness

Common Bird Diseases

Feather Loss

Symptoms:

  • Missing feathers, particularly on the wings and tail
  • Excessive preening or scratching
  • Skin irritation or sores

Beak Mites

Symptoms:

  • Flaky, dry skin around the beak
  • Beak deformities
  • Excessive scratching or rubbing of the beak

Recommended Exercises for Birds

1. Wing Flapping

This exercise is great for strengthening the wings and improving overall health.

Duration: 5-10 minutes a day

2. Climbing and Perching

Encourages natural behavior and helps in improving the bird's leg strength.

Duration: 15-20 minutes a day
