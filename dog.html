Dog Health Problems & Exercises

Arthritis (Joint Pain)

Symptoms:

  • Lameness or limping
  • Difficulty standing up or lying down
  • Stiffness, especially after resting
  • Decreased activity or reluctance to exercise
  • Swelling or heat around the joints

Solution: Pain relief medication, joint supplements, physical therapy, and weight management.

Basic Dog Exercises

1. Walking

This is the most basic and effective exercise for dogs. Regular walks help to improve joint mobility, cardiovascular health, and prevent obesity.

How to Perform:

  • Start with a slow pace and gradually increase speed as your dog gets more comfortable.
  • Walk for about 20-30 minutes, depending on your dog's age and energy level.

2. Fetch

Fetch is a fun and engaging exercise for your dog. It helps improve muscle strength, agility, and cardiovascular fitness.

How to Perform:

  • Find a safe, open space with no hazards (like traffic or dangerous objects).
  • Throw a toy or ball and encourage your dog to chase and return it.
  • Repeat the game for 15-20 minutes, taking breaks in between to avoid over-exertion.
